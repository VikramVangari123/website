Augmented Reality on Web

Deliver immersive experiences on mobile

Augmented reality experiences, for a long time, have relied on the need for native applications that demand dedicated resources to build and occupy high memory in the device of the user. There is no incentive for users to download and keep the app after experiencing the content.

Web-based augmented reality removes this friction in the adoption of AR and allows you to publish AR experiences that run on mobile browsers across devices. WOWSOME helps you craft a customized Web AR experience for your target group and deliver it via a URL which can be shared across multiple channels.

Visit this URL on your smartphone browser and follow the instructions to see the demo of Web AR for a 3D product visualization in your environment.
